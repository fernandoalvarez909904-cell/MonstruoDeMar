About Us

Welcome to Monstruo de Mar, where every dish is a marine experience. Ceviches, shrimp, octopus, and much more, with the authentic flavor of the Pacific. Nestled in Mazatlán's Golden Zone, we source the freshest seafood to create exquisite, sustainable dishes in an elegant setting.
